Our Story

FromSeed was born from a passion for wholesome food and a desire to create a haven for food enthusiasts. As you step into our cozy space, you’ll feel the embrace of warm wood textures, soft lighting, and the gentle hum of conversations. It’s a place where time slows down, and every meal becomes a cherished memory.

The FromSeed Experience

Breakfast:

Rise with the sun and savor our house-made granola, topped with sun-kissed berries and drizzled honey. Pair it with a freshly brewed cup of organic coffee—the perfect start to your day.

Lunch:

Our open-faced sandwiches are a canvas of colors and textures. Try the avocado toast adorned with edible flowers or the roasted vegetable tartine. And don’t miss our signature cold-pressed juices—they’re like sips of liquid sunshine.

Dinner:

As twilight settles, indulge in our tasting menu. Each course tells a chapter of our culinary journey. The beetroot carpaccio whispers of soil and rain, while the wild-caught salmon sings of distant oceans.
